Designers Brands that has participated in our Events

In view of our specialty in organization and promotion of events, Green Waves in partnership with Trans African Centre for Development (TRACED) Maputo held the first edition of AFRICAN WOMEN DESIGNERS WEEK in Radisson Blu Hotel, Maputo Mozambique, Themed “FASHION AND THE SOCIETY” with the aim of addressing and raising awareness in the society at large on the issue of GENDER BASED VIOLENCE in African Societies. In participation were designers from over 11 African Countries inlcuding Bostwana, Swaziland, Angola, Nigeria, South Africa, Guinea bissau, Zimbabwe, Mozambique etc.
